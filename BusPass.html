ABOUT

Police Personnel and Soldiers felicitated with Gallantry Award and natives of Uttar Pradesh are allowed for free travel facility within the state in UPSRTC buses. Gallantry award holders are allowed for travel facility on the basis of "Pass" issued by the UPSRTC Headquarters. In this travel facility, only Corporation fare is being waived off, Additional Taxes etc. have to be paid by the concerned passenger as per the rules.
